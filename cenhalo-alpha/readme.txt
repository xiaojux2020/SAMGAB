The files in this directory are tidal anisotropy alpha_1 and alpha_0.3 for the centrals or FOF haloes in Guo2011 SAM. 
The columns are:
1. galaxyID
2. fofID
3. alpha_1_1.25 (alpha_1 with smoothing radius 1.25)
4. alpha_1_2.5
5. alpha_1_5
6. alpha_1_10
7. alpha_0.3_1.25 (alpha_0.3 with smoothing radius 1.25)
8. alpha_0.3_2.5
9. alpha_0.3_5
10.alpha_0.3_10
